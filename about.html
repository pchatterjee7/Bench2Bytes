About Me

With a Ph.D. in bioinformatics from Georgia Tech and over 10 years of experience, I lead projects that bridge molecular biology and computational genomics. As the Bioinformatics Team Lead at Leidos, I collaborate with the CDC to optimize data pipelines, mentor scientists, and support research in public health. My expertise focuses on advancing cell and gene therapy through genomic technologies, specializing in single-cell RNA and multi-omics data for therapeutic applications and clinical data analysis.
